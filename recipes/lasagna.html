Lasagna Primovera

This is my favorite comfort food meal for those cold days when your at home under the blanket watching your favorite Godfather movie!

Below are a list of ingredients needed:

    1 pound lean ground beef
    1 pound lean ground beef
    1 medium yellow onion, chopped
    2 teaspoons Parsley Flakes
    2 teaspoons Basil Leaves
    2 teaspoons Oregano Leaves
    1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
    1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
    1 teaspoon salt
    2 cans of Crushed Red Tomatoes
